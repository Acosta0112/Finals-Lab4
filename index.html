Call of Duty Clan Wars

Call of Duty Clan Wars are a weekly, competitive event where clans battle each other for territory by completing challenges to earn points. Members contribute by completing specific in-game tasks on different nodes of a map, and the clan that accumulates the most points controls the nodes and earns rewards. The results of these wars place clans into leagues, and performance can be improved through various strategies, like focusing on a primary target and playing with clanmates. .

How to join clan?

To join a clan in Call of Duty (COD), you typically navigate to the in-game clan menu and search for active clans. The exact steps and screen layouts may differ slightly depending on the specific Call of Duty game you are playing (e.g., COD: Mobile, Warzone, etc.).

Call of duty Clan Championship

The Call of Duty Championship is the annual, high-stakes professional tournament that determines the world champion team for a specific Call of Duty season. This event involves top professional teams qualifying through various regional and seasonal event s (such as the Call of Duty League or CODM World Championship stages) to compete for a substantial prize pool.
